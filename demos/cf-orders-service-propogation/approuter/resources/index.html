Every API call here performs an On-Behalf-Of token exchange before querying HANA. The backend calls req.authInfo.requestTokenForClient(hana, null) to get a JWT scoped for the HANA service. HANA connects under your identity — not a service account.

Watch the created_by field in POST /orders responses — it shows CURRENT_USER as recorded by HANA, proving which identity made the connection.
